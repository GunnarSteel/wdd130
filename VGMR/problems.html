Each time we are presented with a problem-solving condition within a game we must decide what the best path is for our character. Each path can be different swaying the way our game ends. Problem solving helps our brains memory retention by creating those scenarios and allowing us to play them in our head on how we want to play. This helps our memory because it allows us to look back on different decisions, good and bad allowing us to change the path to best fit our circumstance in the real world. Within the real world we are faced with many challenges that use problem-solving. Each of those challenges have been made a little easier for those who play video games because we are able to notice the challenge quicker and are able to solve it in a matter that allows the outcome of who ever is involved to be okay in the end.
