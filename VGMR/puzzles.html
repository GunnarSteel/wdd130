Puzzles within video games help us within our lives and with our memory by creating and improving a skill that is used in many different environments. Many of these environments include School, work, and social spaces. This skill of solving puzzles help individuals navigate through different situations and allow our brains to create a map of our surroundings that you are able to piece together so that you do not get lost. All puzzles are not the same which is why video games vary there puzzles as well. Allowing each players brain to be tested to create a better understanding for each encounter.
